OUR STORY

We started Dobaara because nothing good should go to
waste.

Every year, millions of perfectly good clothes are thrown away. Not because they're broken — just forgotten. We collect them, care for them, and bring them to people who love real style at honest prices.

"Dobaara — दोबारा — means once more. That's exactly what we do."
